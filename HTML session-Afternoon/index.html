Hospital Management Web Application
Project I

The aim to develop an application of Hospital Management System for “Memorial Hospital” is to keep the record of patients, doctors, and donor details in a centralized database.This application allows the patients as well as doctors to log into the site, view and handle their scheduled appointments.Scheduled appointments are appended onto the database and the Prescriptions and reports for the same are uploaded by the doctor after the consultation. The patients can hence access all their medical records including prescriptions in a convenient manner.
